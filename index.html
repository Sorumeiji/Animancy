Our Process

01. Strategy

We first focus on getting to know about your dream and idea. Sitting with you, talking about content helps us to make some bid design decisions before getting to the visuals

02. Design

After gathering the content, we form a design. We conduct research based on the content and provide design mockups to narrow down the website you want.

03. Development

Upon choosing the design you love creatintg your website and insert your requested features, and concepts.

04. Support & Training

Lastly, we sit down and talk about how to use your website, such as how to go about updating the website, or helping to plan a launch date that works within your favor.

What We Do

Data Intergration

If you want to display information, redeems, top cheer, etc. We can intergrate that data using various API's such as Twitch, Streamlabs, Muxy, and StreamElements.

OBS GraphicS & Effects

We use NodeCG to create broadcast graphics. This service allows one to run complicated parts of their stream smoothly, and we also can provide subtle animations for sub alerts, and social media callouts.
